Now before you start, make sure you have all these ingridents.

  • Eight beef patties 8oz each
  • 40 slices of bacon
  • 16 slices of cheese
  • 2 Burger Buns
  • Chili 10 oz
  • 16 Sliced tomatoes
  • 35 Sliced onions
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard (optional)
  • Lard (optional, for cooking)
  1. Prepare the patties: Form the eight beef patties. You can optionally add lard to the patties for extra richness, as the restaurant does.
  2. Grill the components: Grill the eight beef patties to your desired doneness. Cook the 40 slices of bacon until crispy. Grill the buns, if desired.
  3. Assemble the burger: Place the bottom bun on your plate. Start layering: Add a patty, then slices of cheese, bacon, tomatoes, and onions. Add chili and other condiments as you go. Continue layering until you have used all eight patties. Place the top bun on the finished burger.
  4. Serve: The burger is ready to be served, but be aware of its extreme size and calorie content.
